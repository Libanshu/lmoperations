About Us

LM Operations provides experienced, reliable support to water and wastewater treatment facilities when staffing challenges arise. We specialize in short-term relief, emergency coverage, commissioning support, and operational optimization — particularly for remote and mine-affected water systems.

With hands-on experience across multiple treatment technologies, we integrate seamlessly into existing operations while maintaining safety, compliance, and performance standards.
